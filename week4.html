Noise during Recording

  • Acoustic noise
  • Electrical noise

Acoustic Noise

The noise of the space and everything that comes into it

How to reduce

  • Listen carefully to the "silence" or "room tone" to identify noise
  • Move away from noisy sources like fans and windows
  • Create an isolated space for recording
  • Turn off noisy sources like A/C, fans, heating, TV, and appliances

Electrical Noise

The noise that get picked up in all the gear, and that gear itself creates

How to reduce

  • Use fewer pieces of gear
  • Use shorter cables
  • Use balanced cables
  • Turn off appliances and dimmers
  • Use high quality gear

Also avoid unnecessary gain

  • Boost level electrically as little as possible
  • Try to move mic closer to the sourse instead of increasing gain
  • Choose a directional microphone to isolate the source from a noisy environment
  • Turn off appliances and dimmers
  • Use high quality gear
