Modulated Effects

  • Chorus
  • Flanger

Flanger

  • A comb filter
  • A slight deley
  • Be put into motion by a low frequency oscillator

Phaser

  • Deep notches in motion
  • Notches are different in left and right
  • Provide a swirling stereo effect

Difference

  • Flanger looks just like the comb filter
  • Flanger always introduce new higher notches when the frequency goes down
  • Phaser allows to change the number of notches
