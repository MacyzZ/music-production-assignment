There are three categories

Related to three of the principles of sound

Dynamic Effects

Related to amplitude

Automatically control volume based on the material over time

Effects

Compressors, limiters, expanders and gates

Delay Effects

Related to the propagation principle of sound

Slight delays to the signal, which tends to represent spaces that you're inside

Effects

Reverbs, delays, phasers, flangers and choruses

Provide a sense of the space

Filter Effects

Related to timbre of sound

Effects

High pass, low pass and band pass, parametric EQ and graphic EQ
